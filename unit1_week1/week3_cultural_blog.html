Pair Programming

Let's answer some questions about pair programming!

Pairing:

>What was it like to pair with others to solve challenges?

I had a great time pairing with others. I love collaborating with people, and so working on a problem together with others is fun for me. It was a great learning experience as well, because I can sometimes fill the gaps in my knowledge with what my partner knows.

>Was something particularly fun or rewarding?

It was rewarding to find a good solution to the problems. The problems weren't super hard, so far, but it was nice to research and implement code that I wasn't super familiar with.

>Was something frustrating and difficult for you?

Nope, not so far.

Feedback:

>How did you feel when you read your feedback?

I didn't get any feedback yet :(.

>Was the feedback you received helpful to your learning?

Not yet.

>Based on the feedback you've received, what are you going to do to improve next time you pair?

N/A

>How was it to write feedback?

Writing feedback has not been very difficult because I've had great partners!

>What was most difficult for you when writing feedback for your pair?

I didn't feel like I could really critique anything about the pair because everything went so smoothly.

>Overall, what do you think of using pairing and feedback to guide your learning?

Great idea, especially for people that like to collaborate creatively. I don't think it always works super well, but when two people are on the same page it's a great use of time.
